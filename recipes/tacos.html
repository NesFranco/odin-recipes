Tacos

Authentic tacos like straight from Mexico

Tacos that'll make you feel like you're in the real Mexico

This recipe was made for people who are looking to try some authentic tacos, like the ones from Mexico

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Steak
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Avocado
  • Salsa
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Cook steak and cut into little pieces
  2. heat up Tortillas
  3. add meat Tortillas
  4. add cilantro and onion
  5. add lime
  6. add avocado and salsa and enjoy
