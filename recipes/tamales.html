Tamales

Tamales made like in Mexico

Tamales as good as the ones in Mexico straight to your dinner table

Prep and cook time are a little high but the wait it totally worth it

Ingredients

  • dried corn husks
  • masa
  • lard
  • salt
  • braised pork
  • sauce

Steps

  1. Spread a corn husk with dough
  2. Spoon small amount of filling
  3. Roll up the husk carefully
  4. Layer tamales in steamer basket
  5. Cover with kitchen towel and lid
  6. Cook for 2 hours
