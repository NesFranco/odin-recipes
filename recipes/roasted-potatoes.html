Roasted Potatoes

The best roasted potatoes you will ever taste

This roasted potatoeshave an special ingredient that makes them stand out from the rest

You will wish you had found these sooner. Lets start with the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • russet potatoes
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • italian seasoning

Steps

  1. cut russet potatoes to small squared
  2. add oil and season to taste
  3. place on tray
  4. cook for 25 minutes
  5. check with a fork to see if they're soft
